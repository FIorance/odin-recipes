Vegetable Pizza

This will be a vegetable pizza, you can omit or add any vegetables you want.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Mozerella Cheese
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, butter, milk, and eggs together in a bowl
  2. Pour the mixture into a pan and let it bake until no batter remains on the toothpick when you stick it in
  3. Take the pizza out, add tomatoe sauce and mozeralla cheese on top, and put it back in the stove until the cheese melts
  4. Take the pizza out and let it cool
  5. Add desired vegetables on top
  6. Put pizza back in fridge until the vegetables warm (optional)
  7. Cut the pizza
