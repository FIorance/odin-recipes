Vanilla Cake

This is going to be a vanilla cake, because vanilla is the best.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Frosting
  • Sprinkles (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, butter, milk, and eggs together in a bowl
  2. Pour the mixture into a pan and let it bake until it passes the toothpick test*
  3. Take the cake out and let it cool
  4. Cut the cake in half horizontally
  5. Heap frosting onto the bottom half and put the top back
  6. Add frosting to the sides and top of the cake
  7. Decorate with sprinkles (optional)

*Toothpick test is where you stick in a toothpick. If batter is on the toothpick when it's pulled out, the batter needs to stay in the oven more. If there is no batter on the toothpick, it's ready.
