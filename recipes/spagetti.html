Spagetti

There are many ways to make spagetti, this one will be baked. It is also a great vegan dish.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoe Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil noodles until the water boils, make sure they don't clump
  2. Mix tomatoe sauce into the noodles until it starts to bubble
  3. Pour the mixture into a pan
  4. Top it with cheese
  5. Bake in the oven until ready
